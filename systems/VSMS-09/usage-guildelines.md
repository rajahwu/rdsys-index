# **6\. Usage Guidelines**

Spacing:

* Base unit: 4px — all spacing is multiples of 4  
* Spacing scale: 4px (xs), 8px (sm), 16px (md), 24px (lg), 32px (xl), 48px (xxl)  
* Content spacing: 16px between related elements, 24px between sections, 32px for major divisions  
* Card padding: 24px for generous breathing room in learning content  
* Container padding: 16px (mobile), 24px (tablet), 32px (desktop)

Corner Radius:

* Small elements: 4px (buttons, tags, badges)  
* Standard UI: 8px (cards, inputs, most surfaces)  
* Large containers: 16px (modals, panels, feature cards)  
* Icons: 2px internal corners

Shadows:

* Subtle elevation:  
  box-shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(28, 44, 75, 0.08);  
  Usage: Cards, gentle lift  
* Medium elevation:  
  box-shadow: 0 4px 12px rgba(28, 44, 75, 0.12);  
  Usage: Hover states, dropdowns  
* Prominent elevation:  
  box-shadow: 0 8px 24px rgba(28, 44, 75, 0.16);  
  Usage: Modals, important panels  
* Focus ring:  
  box-shadow: 0 0 0 3px rgba(167, 138, 85, 0.4);  
  Usage: Keyboard focus (Warm Gold for encouraging feel)

Photography & Imagery:

* Style: Misty mountain photography, ink-wash textures, contemplative landscapes  
* Color treatment: Desaturate to match brand palette, overlay with brand.primary at 5-10% opacity  
* Mood: Calm, elevated, patient — never rushed or dramatic  
* Composition: Horizontal orientation preferred, rule of thirds, negative space for text overlays  
* Do's:  
  * Misty/foggy atmospheres  
  * Layered mountain silhouettes  
  * Soft, diffused lighting  
  * Muted, natural color palette  
  * Contemplative, patient mood  
* Don'ts:  
  * Sharp, dramatic peaks  
  * Harsh lighting or high contrast  
  * Saturated colors  
  * Busy, cluttered compositions  
  * Action shots or high energy

Logo Usage:

* Primary lockup: VSM Peak icon \+ "VSM School" wordmark (horizontal)  
* Icon-only: Peak symbol alone for app icons, favicons  
* Minimum size: 140px width for lockup, 32px for icon  
* Clear space: Maintain padding equal to height of peak on all sides  
* Backgrounds: Works on brand.neutralLight, white, or dark overlays (use inverted colors)  
* Prohibited uses:  
  * Don't stretch or distort the peak shape  
  * Don't rotate (mountain must be upright)  
  * Don't apply effects or filters  
  * Don't use colors outside palette  
  * Don't place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast  
  * Don't crowd with other elements inside clear space
