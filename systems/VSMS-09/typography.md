Type Scale:

* Display: 40px / 1.2 — Marketing hero, major landing pages  
* H1: 32px / 1.3 — Page titles, phase headers  
* H2: 28px / 1.35 — Section headers  
* H3: 22px / 1.4 — Subsection headers, lesson titles  
* H4: 18px / 1.4 — Card titles  
* Body Large: 18px / 1.7 — Lead paragraphs (generous line height for easy reading)  
* Body: 16px / 1.6 — Standard content (optimized for educational reading)  
* Body Small: 14px / 1.6 — Secondary content  
* Caption: 12px / 1.5 — Micro text

Typography Notes:  
Noto Sans Display provides humanist warmth while maintaining professional clarity — perfect for a teaching environment. Inter ensures exceptional readability for body content. Line heights are generous (1.6-1.7 for body text) to support focused reading and learning. Letter-spacing is subtle; labels use slightly looser tracking (+0.05em) for improved legibility at small sizes.

Fallback Stacks:

* Noto Sans Display: 'Noto Sans Display', 'Source Sans Pro', 'Open Sans', sans-serif  
* Inter: 'Inter', \-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, sans-serif
