All colors used in Grindline Studio with complete specifications.

Color Notes:  
Grindline's palette evokes urban energy and street-level hustle. Asphalt Gray (\#2E2E2E) provides gritty, grounded base — the pavement under your feet. Electric Teal (\#00A6A6) brings vibrant, kinetic energy as the primary brand accent. Burnt Coral (\#FF5A5F) creates passionate urgency for CTAs and focus moments. Warm Gold (\#FFC857) celebrates wins and achievements. This palette has more saturation and warmth than Dropframe — less corporate, more human.

Color Pairing Rules:

* Primary brand: grindline.dark \+ grindline.teal  
* High energy: grindline.teal \+ grindline.coral  
* Achievement moments: grindline.gold on grindline.dark  
* Text hierarchy: grindline.dark on neutral.light or neutral.white on grindline.dark  
* CTAs: grindline.coral on neutral.white (high contrast, urgent)  
* Success states: grindline.gold accents
