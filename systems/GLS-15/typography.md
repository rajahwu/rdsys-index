Type Scale:

* Display: 48px / 1.1 — Hero headlines, major street presence  
* H1: 36px / 1.2 — Page titles  
* H2: 28px / 1.25 — Section headers  
* H3: 22px / 1.3 — Card titles  
* H4: 18px / 1.4 — Minor headings  
* Body Large: 18px / 1.6 — Lead content  
* Body: 16px / 1.6 — Standard content  
* Body Small: 14px / 1.5 — Secondary content  
* Caption: 12px / 1.5 — Micro text  
* Buttons: 14-16px / 1 — Uppercase, \+0.05em tracking

Typography Notes:  
Oswald provides condensed, industrial strength for headings — strong, street-level, action-ready. Slightly looser letter-spacing (+0.01em to \+0.02em) prevents feeling cramped despite condensed proportions. Libre Franklin ensures clean, accessible body text. Buttons use uppercase with loose tracking for maximum impact. This combo feels more grounded and urban than Dropframe's geometric precision.

Fallback Stacks:

* Oswald: 'Oswald', 'Inter', 'Franklin Gothic', sans-serif  
* Libre Franklin: 'Libre Franklin', 'Work Sans', 'Open Sans', sans-serif
