# **5\. Illustration Style**

Radiant Systems illustrations are diagrammatic, not expressive.

### **Characteristics**

* Blueprint‑like  
* Wireframe structures  
* Flowcharts  
* Architecture diagrams  
* No characters, no metaphors

### **Palette**

* Cream 50  
* Gray 200  
* Slate 900  
* Teal 500 (sparingly)

### **Use Cases**

* System maps  
* Governance flows  
* Infrastructure diagrams  
* Operational states

### **Never Use**

* Gradients  
* Decorative illustrations  
* Marketing‑style artwork
