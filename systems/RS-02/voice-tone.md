# **8\. Voice & Tone**

Radiant Systems speaks like an operations director:

### **Characteristics**

* Calm  
* Precise  
* Minimal  
* Directive  
* No emotional language

### **Examples**

Correct:  
“System check complete. No anomalies detected.”  
“Token update approved. Propagating changes.”

Incorrect:  
“Everything looks great\!”  
“Nice job\!”
