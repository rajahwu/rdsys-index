Color Notes:  
Dropframe's palette evokes cinematic clarity with a cooler, more technical refinement than Grindline. Cobbs Creek Teal (\#006E6D) provides calm, digital modernity as the primary brand color. Colonial Red (\#C41230) creates urgency and focus for CTAs, echoing the classic teal-and-red cinematic contrast. The three-tier accent system (Warm Gold, Lavender, Slate) adds depth, trust, and polish. Neutral palette ensures clean hierarchy without competing with brand colors.

Color Pairing Rules:

* Primary UI: brand.primary (teal) on neutral.light backgrounds  
* CTAs: brand.secondary (red) for buttons and focus actions  
* Achievement/highlight: tier.one (gold) for success states  
* Depth/polish: tier.two (lavender) for secondary accents  
* Grounded elements: tier.three (slate) for subtle emphasis  
* Text hierarchy: neutral.dark (headings) \> neutral.mid (body)
